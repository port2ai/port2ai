# Delivery Checklist

## Before Starting

- Confirm the exact outcome.
- Confirm access, files, and constraints.
- Save the original state or branch.
- Define what "done" means.

## During Work

- Keep changes scoped.
- Test the core behavior.
- Record decisions that affect the client.

## Before Delivery

- Summarize what changed.
- Note any tests or checks run.
- Mention known limits or follow-up options.
- Provide clear handoff instructions.
